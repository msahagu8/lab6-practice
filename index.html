Welcome to Crater Lakes Visitor’s Guide

Are you visiting Oregon and need ideas of what places to visit? Crater Lake should be one of the places on the top of your list to visit! 

Activities

Crater Lake is the only National Park in Oregon and has the deepest lake in the U.S. with breathtaking views and several outdoor activities

Crater Lake Hiking Trails

Crater Lake has a total of 28 great trails, with 8 easy hiking trails ranging from a mile or less.

  • Duration: Hiking trail times range from 30min to 5hrs
  • Price: Parking $15.00 - $30.00
  • Park Hours of Operation: Open year around 24 hours a day
  • Map

Camping at Crater Lake

Camping in Crater lake is only available from June to late September. Opening and close dates vary depending on the snow level.

  • Fees: Tent Site $21.00 RV No-hookups $31.00 RV Full Hookups $42.00
  • Food Storage: Yes
  • Camping Hours of Operation: June to Late September
  • Mazama Campground
